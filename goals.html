Bringing island energy to SGA

My campaign pillars

Volunteer Island

Service and volunteer opportunities

I want every student to have a chance to get involved, even in small ways, through more accessible service opportunities and stronger participation in events like the canned food drive.

Spirit Island

Engaging events and an active SGA

I don’t want SGA energy to fade after the start of the school year. I want events and momentum to continue through winter and spring too.

Voice Island

Student ideas that shape action

This campaign is about listening as much as leading, so I want regular feedback loops, surveys, and idea collection to become part of how SGA works.

Leadership Island

Prepared, passionate representation

The SGA president represents the student body at meetings, public events, district occasions, and county councils, and I’m ready for that responsibility.
